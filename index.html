What is Solis Labs?

One-person studio

Solis Labs is a studio built around a unified creative workflow. One mind, one vision, full control over image, sound and rhythm.

System thinking

Projects are designed as complete systems. Visuals, animation and audio are developed together to reinforce meaning and mood.

Experimental mindset

Inspired by sci-fi, minimalism and digital industry. Each project is treated as a small universe with its own rules.
